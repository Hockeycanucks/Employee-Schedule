Making a new month

  1. Click the Schedule Options button on the upper toolbar.
  2. Press the New Month option.
  3. Choose the month and type in the year.
  4. Press OK.
  5. After a short period the new month should be created and will be the current sheet.

Settings sheet

  • The Settings sheet contains all the employee names and what days they work.
  • It also contains the store hours.
  • Changing these values will decide who is put on the calendar and what days they work.
  • Make sure the days an employee works are in the correct format as a new month will not be created if there are errors.
  • The validation section will tell the user if there is an error and where.

Adding a new employee to a month

  1. Go to the month the new employee will be added on.
  2. Click on the Schedule Options button on the upper toolbar.
  3. Press the new employee button.
  4. Type in the name of the employee into the dialog box.
  5. Press OK and after a few moments the employee should be added to the month and Settings page.

Adding a Pay Period

  1. For ease of use make sure the schedule is open in Chrome.
  2. Click the Schedule Options button on the upper toolbar.
  3. Press the Add Pay Period button.
  4. Use the date fields to choose the first and last day of the pay period. (Note: if not in chrome use the yyyy-mm-dd format)
  5. Press OK and after a few momnets the next clear pay period at the bottom will be filled with the dates and formulas.
  6. Note: The new period will always show up on the month it ends on.

Removing a Pay Period

  1. Click the Schedule Options button on the upper toolbar.
  2. Hover over the Remove Pay Period button.
  3. Choose the pay period to remove and press the respective button.

Holiday Time Checker

  1. For ease of use make sure the schedule is open in Chrome.
  2. Click the Schedule Options button on the upper toolbar.
  3. Press the Holiday Checker button.
  4. Use the date fields to choose the day starting and end area that needs to be checked. (Note: if not in chrome use the yyyy-mm-dd format)
  5. Press OK and after a few momnets the area maked Holiday Time Checker will display the required formulas.
